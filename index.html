01 Why the humanities matter more

When expression
outpaces interpretation.

When any machine can generate plausible text in any voice at near-zero cost, fluency stops being scarce. What becomes precious is meaning — interpretation, provenance, judgment, the weight of a word across centuries.

❗️ The humanities are not an ornament after technology.They are the disciplines that ask what counts as meaning, whose memory is preserved, and which values are built into our systems.
1
Education

How should students read, write, and judge when machines can speak?

2
Research

How can archives become explorable without losing context?

3
Governance

How can humanistic values shape the AI systems that will shape us?

02 A shift of question

Not "will AI replace us?"  but "what AI do we want to (co-)build?"

The anxious question assumed AI as an oracle that ends the conversation. AI does not replace interpretation, it expands the space of interpretability. We propose three modes of human–AI co-intelligence:

A
Co-reading

AI as research instrument — multiple versions, sources, and annotations read together, never flattened into a single answer.

B
Co-agency

AI as agentic research partner — reader, archivist, critic, builder — working alongside the scholar as a dynamic research team.

C
Co-civilization

AI as humanistic lifeform — CHAI.md: a living constitution that internalizes humanistic values and co-creates with us at civilizational scale.

03 【A. Co-reading】Case study · Cultural semantics across centuries

From words to cultural trajectories.

We use diachronic corpora and computational semantics to trace how culturally dense words move across time, not as isolated dictionary entries, but as fields of interpretation.

情 · qíng

From feeling, affinity, disposition, and circumstance to modern affective and social meanings. The machine draws a semantic trajectory; hermeneutics explains why it matters.

寂寞 · jímò

Loneliness is not only a psychological state. In Chinese textual traditions it passes through Buddhist quietude, poetic desolation, scholar-official frustration, and modern loneliness.

05 【B. Co-agency】CHAI Lab ·

Our local-first AI infrastructure for humanities research

For literature, history, philosophy, religion, and language archives, locality is not only a technical preference. It is a matter of cost, ethics, intellectual autonomy, and cultural trust.

Preserve locallyKeep sensitive and copyrighted corpora within institutional control.
Maintain responsiblyVersion data, annotate provenance, and document interpretive uncertainty.
Explore safelyLet researchers search, compare, and model materials without exporting cultural memory elsewhere.

09 Why this matters beyond research

Sending culture
a delegate.

Soon, much of our institutional life will run on AI whose norms are set elsewhere — in code, policy, defaults. If the humanities are absent from that table, our values go unrepresented.

An AI raised on a CHAI.md is not only the most humanities-literate AI — it can advocate for humanistic value inside the very systems that will govern us. Internalizing culture is step one. Defending it is the point.
10 CHAI.md · Digital living organism

Three organ layers — cognitive core, symbiotic noosphere loop, and governance membrane — breathing together as an awake, human-aligned system.
